About Us

Tayun and Sarah met in high school Latin class. While Latin class was a short-lived endeavor for Tayun they ended up bonding over books and reading so Tayun recommended The Name of the Wind to Sarah. This was back in 2013 when they still had hope that the second book would come out before they graduated from high school.... But here they are, over a decade later in 2024, and the third book has yet to come out.

We are an unserious duo who just wanted to hang out and talk about the book / the series. Hope you enjoy us as we gush over the beautiful literature, amazing world-building, and just have a good time.
